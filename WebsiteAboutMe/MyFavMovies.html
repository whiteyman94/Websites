Man Of Steel

The second movie , Man of Steel, is another adaptation of the comic book superman. It takes a more personal approach to the super hero showing his flaws and inner demons, not just his his immmense super strength and flight. I thought this was an interesting an epic film for the spectacular opening sequence which opens to a civil war on an alien planet and the end battle scene where superman had to kill for the first time in his life to save the people of earth. He will have to live with this decision for the rest of his life and this will show when the sequel will be released.

The Avengers on IMDB

The last film , Avengers Assemble, is about a mix match group of super heroes brought together to save the world from an alien force originating from the other side of the galaxy. This was just a truly incredible film because of the amount of heroes in this film which has never been done before in film history. This film had iron man, who was a man who had a shrapnel in his blood stream which would kill him without the suit, then theres the hulk, who was a man who when he got angry would turn into a huge green rage monster, then theres thor , a norse god who had a hammer forge in the heart of a dying star and the ability to control lightening and then captain americ who was frozen in a block of ice in 1945 and revived in the present day , this character has super strength and a shield that is indestructable.
