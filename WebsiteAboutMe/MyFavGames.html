My Favourite Games

This is a webpage that will describe my favourite Games.

Three of my favourite games are Halo 3, grand theft auto San Andreas and Total War Rome 2. These games are all three different genres of games.

Halo 3 homepage

the first , a first person shooter , the second, a role playing game and the third, a strategy game. Halo is a fantasy sci-fi game set in the year 2555 where space travel exists and the humans have spread across the galaxy. It follows Master Chief, a human who was enrolled in a new program for a new way to fight the enemy, it involves a new combat efficient power armor for fast movement. His companion is an artificial intelligence called Cortana and she is the first of for A.I to be used in such a type of armor. The enemy is a group of aliens ruled by preachers who r trying to destroy the galaxy by switching on the halo rings, which are hugh artificial earths in the shape of a ring.

Grand Theft Auto San Andreas homepage

My second favourite game is an rpg that follows an unfortunate man in the city of Los Angelas during the 1990's. It goes through this characters troubles trying ot survive and how he builds up his fortune and ends up controlling a new gang of the full Los Angelas area. The story changes rapidly and the twists and plots the other characters are shocking for some and others take a pivotal role in the game. These actions affect the charcater and defines him by the end of the game, even ending up having to kill his former mentor and only friend to talk to at the beginning of the game.

Rome 2 homepage

The final game is a strategy gameset in the time of ancient Rome. The map is the whole of the meditarranean, serbia, asia, the top half of Africa, Europe, and Britain. The characters include the britains , barbarians and Romans. This game includes many political matters to deal with including the use of diplomats to the spying of other contries forces and even trade agreements with peacetreaties and client states. The gameplay is based around commanding an empire and fighting in huge battles using general commands to out manover the other players army and beat then to the point where they cannot sustain there empire. This is why its my third favourite game to play.
